# Capstone Proposal / Planning Document — Required Structure

The components a Gauntlet capstone proposal (and the Jun 17 Project Planning Document) should cover.

- **The problem** — what you're solving and why it's technically hard (ambition is the scoring criterion, not polish)
- **Project direction** — which of the three it fits: ML + LLM, a philosophically different agent design, or one problem attacked many ways
- **Technical approach** — the core architecture and how AI functions as a primitive, not a bolt-on
- **Scope** — what "working" looks like in two weeks; the ambitious version vs. the minimum shippable cut
- **Why it's compelling** — the hook that makes others want to join your team (selected proposers recruit teams of 3–4)
- **Hard parts / risks** — the technical bet you're making and where it could break
- **What you'd demo** — a basic sketch of the live 10-minute showcase on Jun 29
