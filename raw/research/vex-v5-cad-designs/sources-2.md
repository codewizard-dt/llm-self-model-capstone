## Excerpts

### S18 — VEX V5 Build Instructions (Hero Bots)
https://www.vexrobotics.com/v5/downloads/build-instructions
> "Hero Bot for the 2024-25 VEX Robotics Competition, High Stakes. 3D Build Instructions > PDF Build Instructions >" — every Hero Bot lists only 3D/PDF instructions and a Parts List; no assembly CAD download.

### S19 — Introduction to Dex
https://kb.vex.com/hc/en-us/articles/40241402832660-An-Introduction-to-Dex-The-V5RC-Push-Back-Hero-Bot
> "Each year, a Hero Bot is designed from the V5 Competition Starter Kit... Hero Bots are designed using the Competition Kit pieces in a way that the robot can be disassembled at the end of the season and made into the next year's Hero Bot with the same Kit."

### S21 — VEXcode VR Axel robot details
https://api.vex.com/vr/home/playgrounds/v5rc_high_stakes/robot_details.html
> "The V5RC Virtual Skills - High Stakes Playground uses the standard VEX V5 Hero Bot, Axel. The Axel VEX V5 Hero Bot measures approximately 350 millimeters long and 280 millimeters wide. Axel is equipped with two sets of Motor Groups for its Arm and Pusher."

### S22 — Getting Started: High Stakes Robot Design
https://kb.vex.com/hc/en-us/articles/28553868379924-Getting-Started-with-V5RC-24-25-Robot-Design-High-Stakes
> "The combined power of all motors (11W & 5.5W) must not exceed 88W. The robot only can use V5 System parts. There is a nearly infinite combination of the parts and connection patterns provided in your VEX V5 Competition Starter Kit."

### S23 — GrabCAD Vex V5 Override
https://grabcad.com/library/vex-v5-override-1

### S24 — GrabCAD Vex Super Kit Cad Files
https://grabcad.com/library/vex-super-kit-cad-files-1

### S25 — GrabCAD RBE1001 VEX V5
https://grabcad.com/library/rbe1001-vex-v5-1
> "VEX V5 Robot Models for WPI RBE1001, as well as other misc models such as the field for the robot. 203 Downloads. Software: SOLIDWORKS, STEP / IGES, 3D Manufacturing Format. Files (262)."

### S27 — Onshape forum: VEX V5 part library
https://forum.onshape.com/discussion/13991/using-onshape-for-vex-robotics-class
> "The VEXU Robotics team I'm on keeps our VEX V5 part library here: [cad.onshape.com shared link]... we imported most of the parts from an existing Autodesk inventor library, then fine tuned things from there. Some components, like c-channels, were built natively in order to increase performance in large assemblies."

### S28 — VEX Forum: Onshape Design Reveal (Push Back)
https://www.vexforum.com/t/onshape-design-reveal-of-our-team-for-push-back/140098
> "Drivetrain: -6 11w Motors (geared to 450) -4 3.25\" Omni-Wheels -2 3.25 Traction Wheels -3 Odometry Pods... Intake: -2 11w Motors (1:1) -Linear-type Intake -Aligning mech -Descore Mech -Color Sort Mech." / "Unfortunately I dont think I will be sharing the actual file for our robot."
