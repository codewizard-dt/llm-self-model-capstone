# Research: VEX V5 Classroom Starter Kit

> The VEX V5 Classroom Starter Kit (SKU 276-7010) is a $849.49 single-robot STEM teaching kit for grades 9–12. It contains a complete V5 electronics bundle plus structural, mechanical, and hardware parts to build the V5 Clawbot. It pairs with the free VEXcode programming environment and the STEM Labs curriculum. This report captures the full product page content plus every internal and external link found on the page.

### What the Kit Builds [S1]

The kit includes all parts needed to build the **V5 Clawbot** — a wheeled robot with a motorised claw arm. It contains one complete **V5 System Bundle** (276-7000) as its electronics core.

## Recommendation

For a single classroom robotics implementation (grades 9–12) focused on STEM curriculum delivery, the **V5 Classroom Starter Kit (276-7010)** is the entry point. For a full 24–36 student classroom, the **V5 Classroom Starter Bundle (276-7070)** at 6× kits is the recommended purchase. Add the **VEX PD+ licence (210-8353)** for professional development support and consider **VEXcare** extended warranty for institutional use.
