- The very soft foam balls may be poor flywheel test objects because they can absorb energy instead of rebounding. They remain useful for scoop tests and low-risk early feed tests.

## Planning Consequence

The scoop should use the 8 in strap as a clamp spine wherever possible. The flywheel should be drawn and built as two parallel 12 in strap side plates with spacers between them, a perpendicular flywheel shaft through the plates, a wheel centered between the side plates, and an adjustable backplate/chute made from the 8 in strap or other measured material.
