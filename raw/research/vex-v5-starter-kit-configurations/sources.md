> "Robot with a standard drivetrain that can move forward/reverse and turn. This build requires a Classroom Starter or Classroom Super Kit."

### S2 — Building with VEX V5
https://kb.vex.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046701172-Building-with-VEX-V5
> "Whether it will be the V5 Clawbot whose instructions came with the Classroom Starter Kit, one of the many builds found on the VEX Robotics site, or one of the builds found in the STEM Labs; it is recommended to start with a set of instructions in order to familiarize yourself with the VEX V5 system before assembling your first custom-designed robot."
> "The instruction sets within the Builds and the STEM labs should require no modifications to the structural pieces. However, custom-designed robots may require the structural parts to be cut and/or bent into custom pieces."
