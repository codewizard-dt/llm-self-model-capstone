Feature · ros2-align-to-tag

Definition

Prove the first ROS 2 closed-loop robot-control slice for Vexy: calibrated Camera Module 3 frames feed rectified AprilTag pose, the ROS VEX bridge proves heartbeat/stop/motion against the V5 Brain, an `AlignToTag` action sends only short bounded commands until the robot reaches tag-alignment tolerance or reports a typed failure, and the run records both raw MCAP evidence and contract-valid JSONL semantics.
