# Research: UT Austin VEX U Championship Team

Research conducted 2026-06-16 into the people and institutional structure behind UT Austin's VEX U competition team, plus the most relevant UT faculty for the capstone's robot self-model concept. Slug: `ut-vexu-team`.

derived_from::[[ut-ieee-ras]]

## Team & Competition

The winning team is **GHOST** (VEX team ID: 1565X), the VEX U committee of UT Austin's relates_to::[[ut-ieee-ras]]. They won the **2025 VEX AI World Championship** in Houston TX (early June 2025) — fully autonomous, 2-robot format — plus Best Engineering Documentation and Sportsmanship awards. Nine members named in official coverage.

**Tech stack**: VEX V5 Brain + Nvidia Jetson + LIDAR + Intel Realsense camera + ROS + C++ autonomous framework. This is a more sophisticated integration than the capstone plans — it confirms VEX V5 as a viable autonomous research platform.
