# Shape · Ship · Sync

The masterclass's operating framework, credited to uses::[[Lenny Rachitsky]]. **Three jobs, one operator** — everything else is a sub-skill of one of these three.

## 1 — Shape (discover the right problem)

- **Continuous discovery** ([[Teresa Torres]]): one customer conversation per week, not surveys. Three questions: *Walk me through the last time you tried to do X / What were you thinking? / What did you try next?*
- **Opportunity Solution Tree**: outcome → opportunities → solutions → assumption tests. Validate the opportunity before committing to a solution.
- **Competitive analysis**: tier competitors (Direct / Adjacent / Substitutes), then SWOT. Know your defensible advantage.
- **North Star Metric**: pick the one metric whose increase most accelerates the flywheel (6 categories: revenue, customer growth, consumption, engagement, growth efficiency, UX).
- **TAM → SAM → SOM** and unit economics: if **LTV:CAC < 3:1** the business model doesn't work — know this first.
- **Strategy** (Lenny's stack: Mission → Vision → Strategy → Goals → Roadmap → Task), written as Situation / Complication / Resolution; 3–5 pillars max.

## 2 — Ship (validate and execute relentlessly)

- **Ruthless scoping**: cut scope in half, then in half again. "What's the smallest thing I can ship that tells me if this works?"
- **Validate before you build**: Fake Door (test demand), Wizard of Oz (be the algorithm by hand), A/B Slice (cheapest real signal).
- Case: [[Anthropic]] — feedback-to-production in ≤5 days with minimal PM.

## 3 — Sync (communicate context, trade-offs, impact)

- **Communicate toward action**: frame decisions, not constraints. Include impact, specifics, context, trade-off, and an opinion/ask.
- **Accountability loop**: Discover → Ship → Measure (vs. North Star) → Review, every month.
- **Classify every outcome**: Nailed it / Scraped it / Failed it. Honest labels compound into honest judgment.
- Cases: PostHog (cut churn 21%→10% by fixing trust, not adding features) and Duolingo (CURR insight drove 4.5× DAU growth from one correct metric).
