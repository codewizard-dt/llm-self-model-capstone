# UT Austin IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS)

**Student org** at the University of Texas at Austin, founded 1997. One of the largest robotics organizations in UT's Cockrell School of Engineering, with 100+ members across 7 majors (CS, ECE, ME, ASE, COE, and others).

**Website**: ras.ece.utexas.edu  
**Email**: ut.ieee.ras+web@gmail.com  
**GitHub**: github.com/ut-ras (org) | github.com/VEXU-GHOST (VEX-specific)  
**Instagram / YouTube**: @ut_ieee_ras  

## Committees

- **VEX U** (team name: GHOST, ID: 1565X) — 2v2 fully autonomous competition; software-heavy, computer vision, fast mechanical prototyping
- **RoboMaster** — 3v3 FPS-style robot shooter; custom manufacturing, CV, power management
- **Demobots** — creative mechatronic projects (CouchBot, ChessBot, PolargraphBot)
- **Robotathon** — fall competition; students given a kit and build a robot in 2 months
- **IGVC** — Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition

## VEX U / GHOST Championship (2025)

Won the **2025 VEX AI World Championship** in Houston TX. Nine members:

- [[Karmanyaah Malhotra]] (CS '27) — lead programmer
- [[Melissa Cruz]] (ASE PhD) — **outreach target: hardware access**
- [[Maxx Wilson]] (ASE PhD) — **outreach target: hardware access**

**Tech stack**: VEX V5 Brain + Nvidia Jetson + LIDAR + Intel Realsense + ROS2 Humble (Ubuntu 22.04) + C++. Awards also won: Best Engineering Documentation, Sportsmanship.

**Open-source framework**: `github.com/VEXU-GHOST/VEXU_GHOST` — full V5 + Jetson ROS2 stack for path planning, localization, sensor fusion, motion control. Runs unchanged on Raspberry Pi 5 with Ubuntu 22.04 (ROS2 Humble official ARM64 support). relates_to::[[vaic-reference-architecture]] relates_to::[[vex-coprocessor-pattern]]

## Faculty Advisor

No faculty advisor is publicly named. IEEE RAS student branch chapters are formally required to have a faculty advisor per IEEE policy, but it is not listed on any public UT page. Email the org to find out.
