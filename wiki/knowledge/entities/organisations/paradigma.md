# Paradigma

Early-stage company (paradigma.inc) building "the infrastructure for autonomous research." Founded by [[francesco-pappone]] (CEO) and co-founders (email: founders@paradigma.inc). Published the founding thesis essay *"The New Age of Research"* (March 2026) alongside the launch of their product [[flywheel]].

**Core thesis**: progress is bottlenecked by the ability to accumulate and operationalise intelligence; AI moves this bottleneck to compute; when intelligence scales, the systems that *organise* its output become load-bearing. Flywheel is their first product implementing this thesis.

## Real-World Validation

Paradigma sponsored the **HackRome 2026** hackathon and awarded "Best use of Flywheel" to the [[giacomo-rancurel|hyperfamila]] project — an agentic physical autoresearch system using SO101 arms and OpenAI Codex. This confirms Flywheel is production-usable in physical agent loops, not just digital research workflows.
