# Anthropic

AI safety lab and maker of the Claude model family. In the Cohort 5 masterclass, Anthropic is the headline **case study for the [[ai-product-engineer]] role**.

Per [[Cat Wu]] (Head of Product), Anthropic hires "engineers with great product taste who can go from user feedback on Twitter to a shipped product by the end of the week — with almost no product involvement." The deck cites a feedback-to-production cycle time of **≤5 days** with minimal PM headcount, presenting it as the working proof that the PM↔engineer handoff is dissolving.
