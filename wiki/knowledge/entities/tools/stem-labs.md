# VEX STEM Labs

VEX's free, **standards-aligned** curriculum system — hands-on activities (diverse builds, games, competitions) that pair with the physical kits and the relates_to::[[vexcode]] coding environment. STEM Labs were designed by classroom teachers and cognitive scientists working with the Carnegie Mellon Robotics Academy, and are organised into scaffolded units with a 1:1 pacing guide that maps lab units to VEXcode VR activities.

For the relates_to::[[vex-v5]] platform, STEM Labs are the recommended curriculum companion to the Classroom Starter Kit — see derived_from::[[vex-v5-classroom-starter-kit]]. Educators can integrate them into existing curriculum or use them standalone. Portal: https://education.vex.com/ · supported by VEX Certified Educators (https://certifications.vex.com/educator) and PD+ professional development (https://pd.vex.com/).
