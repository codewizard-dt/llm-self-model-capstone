# Teresa Torres

Product-discovery coach and author of *Continuous Discovery Habits*. The Cohort 5 masterclass draws its "Shape" practices from her work.

Her contributions cited in the deck:
- **Continuous discovery**: the minimum viable practice is *one customer conversation per week* — not surveys or dashboards, but a human asking open-ended questions. Make it a ritual, not an event.
- The three core interview questions: *Walk me through the last time you tried to do X / What were you thinking at that moment? / What did you try next?*
- The **Opportunity Solution Tree** (outcome → opportunities → solutions → assumption tests).
