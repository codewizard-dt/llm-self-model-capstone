# Francesco Pappone

CEO and co-founder of relates_to::[[paradigma]], the company building [[flywheel]]. X handle: `@tensorqt`. Published *"The New Age of Research"* (March 2026, paradigma.inc/blog) as the founding thesis essay alongside Flywheel's launch. The essay argues that when intelligence is no longer the bottleneck, **the systems that organise its output become load-bearing** — and that Flywheel's DAG model is the right substrate for that organisation.
