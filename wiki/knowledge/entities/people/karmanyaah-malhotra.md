# Karmanyaah Malhotra

**CS '27**, University of Texas at Austin.  
**GitHub**: [karmanyaahm](https://github.com/karmanyaahm) | **Website**: karmanyaah.malhotra.cc

Lead programmer for the GHOST VEX U team (relates_to::[[ut-ieee-ras]]). Won the 2025 VEX AI World Championship. Technical focus: motion planning, IPC, Linux/hardware integration, trajectory planning, ROS. Formerly worked on UnifiedPush (network protocols) and Hylio (UAS / PCB design + firmware).

Quoted in the Texas Robotics championship coverage: *"We entered the most stressful match I have ever experienced in my robotics and sports career."*

Member of VEXU-GHOST (github.com/VEXU-GHOST) and ut-ras (github.com/ut-ras) organizations.
