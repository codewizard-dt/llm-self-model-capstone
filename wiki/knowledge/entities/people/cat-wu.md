# Cat Wu

Head of Product at [[Anthropic]]. Quoted in the Cohort 5 masterclass as primary evidence for the [[ai-product-engineer]] thesis.

Her quote (via Lenny's Podcast, 2025): *"We hire engineers with great product taste who can go from user feedback on Twitter to a shipped product by the end of the week — with almost no product involvement."* The masterclass uses this to argue engineers should "see a problem, ship the answer, and not wait for a spec."
